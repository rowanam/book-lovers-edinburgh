Our Top Three Favourite Stores

Topping's

Best for a cozy cuppa

Topping's is a wonderfully comfortable bookstore located not far from the centre of Edinburgh. With its maze-like warren of bookshelves meandering across two floors, cozy corners and the chance to have a cup of tea, you'll want to spend all afternoon here.
Find this store on Google Maps

Lighthouse

Best for independent voices

Lighthouse is a queer-owned and woman led independent community bookshop. They are an unapologetically activist, intersectional, feminist, antiracist, lgbtq+ community space, and celebrate diversity of thought and expression, championing voices from the margins. In 2020 they were nominated Scotland's Best Independent Bookshop.
Find this store on Google Maps

Golden Hare Books

Best for events and workshops

At Golden hare Books you'll find a knowledgeable team of self-confessed reading addicts who have been selling beautiful and important books since 2012. The charming independent bookshop is situated in Edinburgh's Stockbridge, where you can find an ever-changing collection of fiction and non-fiction for readers of all ages.
Find this store on Google Maps
