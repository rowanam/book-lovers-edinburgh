Favourite Recommendations

Looking for your next read? Check out our favourite Scottish books from Edinburgh and beyond.

Set in Edinburgh

The Fair Botanists by Sara Sheridan

Suffused with the heady aroma of Enlightenment Edinburgh, Sheridan's vibrant period novel centres on the city's enticing botanical gardens and two fascinating women who meet there.

Edinburgh Author

Hamnet by Maggie O'Farrell

One of our greatest living novelists resurrects the short life of Hamnet Shakespeare, in a lyrically written and emotionally devastating account of the Bard's only son.

Scottish Literature

Young Mungo by Douglas Stuart

An emotionally powerful and compassionate portrayal of sexuality and community set in urban Glasgow, as two young men on either side of a sectarian divide start to develop feelings for each other.

Edinburgh Bookstores

Edinburgh is an incredibly literary city, and as such it's no surprise that there are plenty of cozy bookshops to be found scattered about.

Whether you're looking for

  • a comfortable nook to while away a rainy day with a coffee or tea
  • a buzzing store full of other people browsing for new reads
  • an indepedent hole-in-the-wall

or anything in between, we'll point you to our favourite shops in the city for these and more.
