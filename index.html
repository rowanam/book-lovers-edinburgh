Favourite Recommendations

Looking for your next read? Check out our favourite books from previous meetings.

In Arabian Nights

by Tahir Shah

In this entertaining and penetrating book, Tahir sets out on a bold new journey across Morocco that becomes an adventure worthy of the mythical Arabian Nights.

The Thursday Murder Club

by Richard Osman

Laced with killer one-liners and artfully constructed twists, the debut novel from the telly puzzle favourite centres on a posse of pensionable Poirots investigating the suspicious demise of a property developer.

Small Pleasures

by Clare Chambers

A frustrated reporter and a potential Virgin birth inform the subtly absorbing narrative of Small Pleasures; a delicate, tender story about belief, credulity and tilting at happiness.

Edinburgh Bookstores

Edinburgh is an incredibly literary city, and as such it's no surprise that there are plenty of cozy bookshops to be found scattered about.

Whether you're looking for

  • a comfortable nook to while away a rainy day with a coffee or tea
  • a buzzing store full of other people browsing for new reads
  • an indepedent hole-in-the-wall

or anything in between, we'll point you to our favourite shops in the city for these and more.
